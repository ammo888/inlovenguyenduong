On May 4th, 2024

How we met

January, 2018


Our journey began in 2018 when fate brought us together through our shared passion for advocacy and community organization. We were both university students with a burning desire to make a positive impact on the world. Little did we know that our paths were about to merge in the most serendipitous way.

A mere month after our encounter, destiny led us to reconnect in the charming city of Ann Arbor, Michigan. It was there that we took a leap of faith, realizing that our connection was something truly special. From that moment on, our connection deepened, and our love story began to unfold. For the next two months, we were practically inseparable, cherishing every moment together.

But, like all great stories, our romance faced a temporary separation. We continued on our journey under the challenge of long-distance love, as Phat was stationed in South Korea. Despite the physical separation, our hearts remained deeply connected, proving that love knows no boundaries. This chapter of our story, marked by time zones and miles apart, only served to strengthen our commitment to one another. However, distance couldn't diminish the flame of our love; it only fueled our commitment to making our love story the best it could be.

The Proposal


Exactly two years from the day we officially began our journey as a couple. The desire to commemorate this significant milestone with a moment that would forever etch our love had been growing.

Choosing the Maymont Japanese Garden as the backdrop for this momentous occasion was a thoughtful decision. The garden's tranquil beauty and serene ambiance perfectly set the stage for the next chapter in our story. Hand in hand, the gentle sound of the waterfall served as a soothing soundtrack for the moment that would change our lives forever.

As we reached a bridge beneath the cascade of water, we could feel the weight of the moment. Taking a deep breath, Phat got down on one knee and, with a racing heart, asked the question that would bind us together for life. The world seemed to stand still as the words left his lips, and the anticipation of the answer was almost unbearable. With Ashley's resounding "yes," our and journey together continued with renewed purpose and commitment.

What made this moment even more special was the presence of our dear friends. They were hidden in nearby bushes, waiting to share in our joy. As they emerged to celebrate with us, the air was filled with the laughter and love of those who had been witnesses to our journey from the very beginning. Our friends’ support and presence underscored the depth of our connection added an extra layer of joy and meaning to this unforgettable moment.

Our proposal was more than just a question and an answer; it was a promise to continue writing our love story, hand in hand, embracing all the beauty, adventure, and love that the world could offer. The next chapter of our love story had begun, and we couldn't wait to see what the future held for us as a newly engaged couple.
